Description¶

These functions are wrapper functions of MPI_Sendrecv, where information are communicated between two processes.

They are mainly used to exchange the halo cell information, which is needed to evaluate the derivative at the edge of the domain; For instance, second-order derivative of \(\ux\) in \(y\) direction at \(\left( i, 1 \right)\) is computed as:

\[\vat{\frac{\delta^2 \ux}{\delta y^2}}{i,1} = \frac{1}{\Delta y^2} \vat{\ux}{i,0} - \frac{2}{\Delta y^2} \vat{\ux}{i,1} + \frac{1}{\Delta y^2} \vat{\ux}{i,2},\]

where obviously information from the lower process \(\vat{\ux}{i,\text{jsize}}\) is needed. From the lower process’ point of view, \(\vat{\ux}{i,\text{jsize}+1}\) is needed, which is \(\vat{\ux}{i,1}\) of the current process.
