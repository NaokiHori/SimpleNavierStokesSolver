param/estimate_cost.c¶

Estimate computational costs (in wall time) for all explicit and implicit combinations of the diffusive treatments.

Description¶

Sometimes implicit treatments of the diffusive terms are favorable to reduce the computational cost (by increasing the time step size \(\Delta t\)). This, however, imposes additional complexities and computational cost (communications among all MPI processes: the most expensive procedure in this project), which are usually non-trivial In particular, communications among all processors (the most expensive process in this process) are essential, which can totally remove the time step advantage. In order to determine whether the implicit treatments are advantageous, this function integrates the governing equations for a short time and quantifies the additional computational cost.
